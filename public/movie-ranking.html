Natalie's Favourite Films

3 Movies i'll Never Tire of Watching

Inception

It's got action, interesting characters, and a story line that intrigues me on every level. I won't let myself watch it too many times as I don't want to runi it! But every time I watch it I find something new. It's super clever and gets you thinking.

The Matrix

There is a theme here with my love of sci-fi. I am a sucker for a film with a clevr concept that takes you on a full journey. The first Matrix is a classic for a reason!

The Holiday

I thought best to mix it up for my last pick. I love all genres when done well, and this is a film that never fails to make me smile at Christmas. I watch it every single year and i'm stil not board. I love the characters, the way all the storylines overlap and come together, and the overall development. A must watch for anyone still yet to do so.
